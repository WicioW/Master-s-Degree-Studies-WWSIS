There is giver permutation of numbers in array {1,2, ... , n}. Count how many inversions
are in this array(pair of numbers a and b that a>b and a is before b in array.

In:
Number n (1<=n<=10^6)
Permutation of n numbers, separated by single space.
Out:
Number of count of inversions in given permutation.

Example:
In:
6
4 3 1 6 5 2
Out:
8
