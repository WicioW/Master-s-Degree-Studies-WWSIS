One day Frank, found positive number N on the street.
Frank also likes number 30. He wants to change order of digits in N
to make it divisible by 30. How big number can he get?

In: 
In one line there is a number N having at least 100000 digits.
Out:
Write down the number searched for in the task, 
or -1 if it does not exist.

Examples:
In:
30
Out:
30
_____
In: 
120
Out:
210
_____
In:
2931
Out:
-1
